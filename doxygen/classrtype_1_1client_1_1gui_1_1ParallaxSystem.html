Detailed Description

Manages a multi-layered parallax scrolling background system.

The ParallaxSystem creates an immersive space environment with multiple layers of moving elements that scroll at different speeds to create depth perception. Perfect for space-themed games like R-TYPE.

Layers (back to front):

  1. Gradient background (dark blue to black)
  2. Far stars (slow, small, dim)
  3. Medium stars (medium speed and size)
  4. Near stars (fast, large, bright)
  5. Space debris (rotating foreground elements)
